About Us

A new way to travel around India

India is a land of diversity, where one can experience different cuisines, languages, religions, and lifestyles in different regions


India is a destination for spiritual seekers, who can find peace and enlightenment in places like Dharamshala, Pushkar, Auroville, and the Golden Temple.
India is a biodiversity hotspot with its various ecosystems ranging from the evergreen rain forests in the south to the sands of the west to the marshy mangroves of the east. It is home to a large variety of wildlife, including tigers, leopards, rhinos, elephants, birds and river dolphins.
India also has some of the best locations for nature-based activities, such as trekking, hiking, skiing, paragliding, zorbing, canoeing, kayaking and fishing. Visitors can enjoy the thrill of adventure and the serenity of nature at the same time
