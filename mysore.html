About Mysore

Mysore is the third most populous city in the state of Karnataka, India. It is located in the foothills of the Chamundi Hills about 146 km southwest of Bangalore and spread across an area of 128.42 km² ¹. Mysore is known for its rich history and cultural heritage, including its famous Mysore Palace and other historical landmarks.

Why Visit Mysore?

Mysore is a city for every traveler. This cultural capital of Karnataka not only offers richness of past but also presents a beautiful amalgamation of historical splendor with the scenic beauty of nature which is dotted with lakes, waterfalls and gardens⁶. There are many popular places to see in Mysore, each promising a unique experience to the visitors⁶.

Popular Destinations

  • Mysore Palace - One of the largest palaces in India⁶
  • Karanji Lake - A beautiful lake surrounded by nature¹
  • Devaraja Market - A vibrant market with local produce and goods¹
  • Chamundeshwari Temple - A historic temple dedicated to the goddess Chamundeshwari¹
  • Jaganmohan Palace - An art gallery and museum housed in a historic palace³
  • Jayalakshmi Vilas - A palace turned museum showcasing the history and culture of Mysore⁶
  • Mysore Zoo - A zoo with a wide variety of animals and plants¹
  • Mysore Sand Sculpture Museum - A museum showcasing sand sculptures⁶
