About Mumbai

Mumbai is the capital city of Maharashtra located on the west coast of the Indian peninsula. Named after Goddess Mumba Devi, Mumbai is a diverse city that offers a variety of choices to spend some quality time with your loved ones¹. From beaches to theme parks, from historical sites to religious spots, from shopping centers to a wide array of eateries, Mumbai offers something for everyone³.

Why Visit Mumbai?

Mumbai is known as the 'City of Dreams' and is a place that can feel utterly overwhelming to first-time visitors. The pace is frenetic, the roads frantic, the whole place seeming to pulse with energy⁴. Mumbai is ideal for all kinds of holiday, be it a long break or a weekend getaway or a one day trip to the happening city³.

Popular Destinations

  • Gateway of India - A historic landmark overlooking the Arabian Sea¹
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus - A historic railway station and UNESCO World Heritage Site¹
  • Elephanta Caves - A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring rock-cut temples¹
  • Bandra–Worli Sea Link - A cable-stayed bridge connecting Bandra and Worli¹
  • Mani Bhavan - A museum dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi¹
  • Taraporewala Aquarium - An aquarium with a wide variety of marine life¹
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya - A museum showcasing art and history¹
  • St. Thomas Cathedral, Mumbai - A historic cathedral in Mumbai¹
